About the Database

AASHE's Campus Green Funds Database indexes a variety of green funds that are used to pay for campus sustainability projects. Special thanks to Mieko Ozeki from the University of Vermont Sustainability Office for sharing her resources and expertise in the development of this database. For information on Campus Sustainability Revolving Funds visit that database here.
