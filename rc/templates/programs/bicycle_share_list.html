University bicycle share programs provide free or low cost bikes to students, faculty and staff. Bike share programs increase campus mobility by providing on-demand transportation that is both clean and efficient.

This resource lists existing bike share programs on campuses. Programs are categorized according to whether the program is free for all students, faculty and staff or requires a rental fee. In addition, the University Bike Programs group may also be of interest for those working on bicycling programs on campus.
