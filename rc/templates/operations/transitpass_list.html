Universal Bus/Transit Passes (U-Passes) provide students (and sometimes faculty and staff) with unlimited local transit service 24 hours per day with no fares required. The most common source of funding for U-Pass programs is student fees; however, many programs are also funded by parking fees and general funds. These programs have proved very effective in both increasing public transportation use and decreasing single occupancy vehicle commutes to campus. This resource lists existing U-pass programs on campus. Programs are categorized according to whether the pass entitles the student and/or faculty and staff member to unlimited free rides or a 50% or greater pass discount. Campuses that have been identified by AASHE staff as having particularly informative websites have been highlighted.
