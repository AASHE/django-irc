This resource lists "green" athletic buildings at higher education institutions. In addition to new construction, it includes building renovations as well as buildings being operated or maintained sustainably. Buildings that received a LEED (Leadership in Energy and Environmental Design) certification are listed first, grouped by certification level (Platinum, Gold, Silver, Certified). Buildings certified by systems other than LEED are listed next, followed by green buildings that are not certified. LEED was developed by the U.S. Green Building Council (USGBC). Other green building rating systems include the Green Globe Certification and BREAMM.
